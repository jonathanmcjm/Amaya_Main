Made with Passion. Served with Fire.

At Los Traviesos, we bring chef-driven Mexican street food to the streets of El Paso with bold flavor and creative edge. From crispy birria flautas to our signature quesabirria tacos, every dish is made from scratch and built to satisfy. Family-owned, locally loved, and always cooking with heart — we don’t just serve food, we serve experiences.

Your Next Taco Stop

Our Favorites

Quesabirria Tacos

Crispy, cheesy, slow-roasted birria beef with fresh cilantro and onions, served with a side of consommé.

Birria Flautas

Crispy rolled tacos filled with our house birria, topped with lettuce, tomato, crema, and cheese.

Loaded Fries

A wild mix of creamy elote, cheese sauce, Hot Cheetos, Valentina, and birria — because why not?

Birria Ramen

A street-style twist: ramen noodles cooked in our consommé, topped with juicy birria and a splash of salsa.
